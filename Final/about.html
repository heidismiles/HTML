Hello!

I am so happy to be your teacher this year!

Growing up, I always wanted to be an elementary school teacher. As early as middle school, I would sign up as a teacher’s assistant whenever I could. When I began my college career at CSU, Long Beach, there was no question that I would major in Elementary Education. I earned my Bachelor’s degree and, after that, a California Multiple Subject teaching credential.

Before teaching at Irvine Elementary, I worked as a tutor for a well-known test preparation company. I had a lot of fun helping students ace tests like the SAT, ACT, GRE, and GMAT. And more than ever, I learned that a person can truly accomplish anything if they work hard enough!
