Classroom Supply List

Aug 29, 2016

This year's supply list is below. We ask that each child bring the requested items. If you will have any trouble accomplishing this, please just let me know!

  • Pencils - 20
  • Scissors - Fiskars childrens (left-handed, if needed for your child)
  • Crayola crayons - 24 pack, regular colors
  • Tissues - 2 large boxes, Kleenex or Puffs brand please
  • Copy Paper - 2 reams, 500 pages each, standard white paper
  • Glue Sticks - 6 pack
  • Expo Markers - 4 pack (black, blue, red, green)

In addition, the following items would be very nice to have:

  • Colored copy paper - Pastels or Astrobrights, standard weight
  • Colored pencils or markers - 24 pack, regular colors
  • Crayola crayons - flesh colors
  • Hand sanitizer - large pump bottle
